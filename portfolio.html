About Me

I am a professional photographer and digital artist. I particularly specialise in fine-art contemporary landscape and portrait photography. I have been taking photos ever since I won an instamatic camera at age six. I had the wonderful privilege of growing up in wild, open and unspoilt surroundings. Over the years I've developed a style that pushes the boundary between photography and art. I specialise in landscape and all forms of portrait photography.
